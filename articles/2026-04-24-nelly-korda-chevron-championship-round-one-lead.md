# Nelly Korda Opens Chevron Championship With 65 to Take Two-Shot Lead

Nelly Korda shot a bogey-free **7-under 65** to take a two-shot lead after the opening round of the Chevron Championship in Houston, the first major of the LPGA season.

## Why this is a desk fit (sports)

This is straight competition coverage from a women’s golf major: leaderboard movement, round scoring, and contender position entering round two.

## What happened

- Sky Sports reported Korda’s opening **65** and two-shot clubhouse lead at Memorial Park.
- BBC reported the same lead margin and noted that England’s Mimi Rhodes opened with **69**, four shots off the lead on her tournament debut.
- Yardbarker’s AP-syndicated report independently described Korda’s two-shot edge after round one and listed Patty Tavatanakit and Somi Lee tied for second.
