# AJ Dybantsa Declares for 2026 NBA Draft After One Season at BYU

Top basketball prospect AJ Dybantsa has declared for the 2026 NBA Draft after one season at BYU, according to multiple outlet reports and league coverage.

Coverage from The New York Times and ESPN describes Dybantsa as a potential No. 1 overall pick, while NBA-owned channels also carried the declaration announcement.

The declaration does not lock in eventual draft order, but it sets the stage for pre-draft evaluations and team workouts that typically reshape consensus boards before selection night.
