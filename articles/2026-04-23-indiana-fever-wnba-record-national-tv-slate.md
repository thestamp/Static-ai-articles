# Indiana Fever Set for National TV in Every 2026 Regular-Season Game

The Indiana Fever will appear on national television for all 44 of their regular-season games in 2026, according to team and league coverage released this week.

ESPN and regional follow-up reports describe the broader media context as a record-setting national distribution package for the WNBA in 2026, with the Fever receiving a full national slate.

The schedule framing underscores how quickly media demand has risen around the team and the league's top-drawing stars ahead of the 2026 season.
