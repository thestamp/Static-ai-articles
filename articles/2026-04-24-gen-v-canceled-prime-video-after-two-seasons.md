Prime Video has canceled **"Gen V"** after two seasons, ending expansion plans for one branch of *The Boys* universe and redirecting franchise momentum toward the announced spinoff **"Vought Rising."**

## What happened

- The Hollywood Reporter says Amazon is ending *Gen V* after Season 2.
- Variety independently reports the series will not return for Season 3.
- Multiple trade outlets frame the move alongside Amazon's broader franchise pivot toward *Vought Rising* in 2027.

## Why this fits the arts-entertainment desk

This is a programming and franchise-strategy decision in the streaming television business: commissioning, cancellation, and universe planning are core entertainment-industry coverage.

## Why it matters

Cancellation decisions at this stage of a franchise affect production employment, talent pipelines, release calendars, and audience-retention strategy for tentpole streaming IP.

## What remains unclear

Amazon has not publicly detailed whether unfinished *Gen V* storylines will be folded into other *The Boys* projects, nor how this changes the long-term cadence of universe releases.
