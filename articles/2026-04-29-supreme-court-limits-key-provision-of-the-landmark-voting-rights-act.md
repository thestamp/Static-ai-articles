## What happened
World Athletics rejected 11 applications for athletes to switch allegiance to Turkey ahead of the Los Angeles 2028 Olympic cycle, according to multiple reports carried on April 16-17.

## Why it matters
Nationality-transfer rules can reshape medal prospects and roster strategy in global track and field. A rejection at this scale is a major setback for Turkey's short-term Olympic talent strategy and signals strict enforcement by governing bodies.

## What to watch
- Whether Turkey or affected federations pursue appeals or revised filings.
- How World Athletics communicates precedent for future transfer cases before LA 2028 qualification accelerates.
- Competitive impact for sprint and relay events tied to the blocked transfers.
