# Sara Cox Replaces Scott Mills on BBC Radio 2 Breakfast Show After Sudden Exit

BBC Radio 2 has named Sara Cox as the new presenter of its breakfast show, replacing Scott Mills after his abrupt departure this week.

The BBC report says Cox, already a familiar voice on the station's daytime schedule, will move into the flagship morning slot as the network stabilizes programming after the change.

Independent coverage from *NME* similarly reports that Cox will take over the breakfast role and describes the change as coming after allegations tied to Mills' exit, while key details around any formal findings remain limited in public reporting.

What is confirmed now is the lineup decision itself; unresolved elements concern the full circumstances surrounding the prior host's departure.
