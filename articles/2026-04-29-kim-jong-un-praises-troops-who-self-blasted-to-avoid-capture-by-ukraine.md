North Korean leader Kim Jong Un praised troops he said fought alongside Russian forces and described instances of soldiers choosing death over capture, according to North Korean state messaging cited in BBC reporting.

The development adds to evidence that North Korean personnel are being drawn into the war theater linked to Russia's campaign in Ukraine, a cross-border security issue with implications for sanctions, escalation risk, and regional diplomacy.

Public details remain limited and many battlefield claims are difficult to independently verify in real time. Officials and monitors have urged caution as assessments continue.

BBC reported the remarks in coverage published on 2026-04-29, citing state-linked accounts and ongoing scrutiny from international observers.
