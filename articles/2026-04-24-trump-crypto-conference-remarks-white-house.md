# Trump to Address Crypto Conference Saturday, White House Says

President Donald Trump is scheduled to deliver remarks on Saturday to a cryptocurrency conference, according to Reuters reporting that cites the White House. The planned appearance signals continued political engagement with digital-asset constituencies during the 2026 campaign cycle.

Reuters frames the appearance as an official White House-confirmed schedule item. Broader coverage from BBC also indicates Trump has been actively courting crypto-aligned voters and donors, adding campaign context to the policy and messaging significance of the event.

## Why this matters

A presidential appearance at a crypto-focused event can shape expectations around digital-asset regulation, enforcement posture, and market sentiment. Even without immediate policy announcements, high-visibility signaling from the White House can influence lobbying priorities and political narratives around financial innovation.

## What to watch next

- Whether the remarks include specific regulatory commitments on securities classification, exchange oversight, or stablecoins.
- Whether campaign and administration messaging align on crypto policy language after the event.
- Any market reaction in major crypto assets and related public companies following the speech.
