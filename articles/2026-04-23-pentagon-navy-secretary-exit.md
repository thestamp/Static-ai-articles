# Pentagon Says Navy Secretary John Phelan Is Leaving Immediately

The Pentagon said Thursday that Navy Secretary John Phelan is departing his post effective immediately, a leadership change that arrives amid continued pressure on U.S. defense priorities and budget planning.

According to public reporting, no long transition window was announced, and defense officials indicated a rapid handoff to interim leadership while the administration prepares next personnel steps.

## Why this matters

- Leadership turnover at the service-secretary level can affect near-term procurement, readiness, and congressional engagement.
- A same-day departure compresses transition timelines for pending Navy policy decisions.
- The move lands during an active cycle of defense oversight and appropriations scrutiny.
