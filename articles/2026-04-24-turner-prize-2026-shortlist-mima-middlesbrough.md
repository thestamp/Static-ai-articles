# Turner Prize 2026 Shortlist Names Four Artists, With Exhibition Set for MIMA in Middlesbrough

Tate Britain announced the four artists shortlisted for the Turner Prize 2026: Simeon Barclay, Kira Freije, Marguerite Humeau, and Tanoa Sasraku.

According to Tate’s press release, the shortlist exhibition will run at MIMA (Middlesbrough Institute of Modern Art) from 26 September 2026 to 29 March 2027, with the winner scheduled to be announced on 10 December 2026.

The Guardian’s coverage of the shortlist independently identifies the same four artists and reports the 2026 prize cycle as a Middlesbrough-hosted edition, reinforcing the venue and shortlist framing.

At filing time, the confirmed facts are the named shortlist and the announced exhibition/winner timeline. Jury deliberation outcomes and any exhibition-program changes remain pending.
