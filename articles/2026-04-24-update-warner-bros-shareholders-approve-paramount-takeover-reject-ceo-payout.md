# Update: Warner Bros. Discovery Shareholders Approve Paramount Takeover, Reject CEO Payout Proposal

Warner Bros. Discovery shareholders have now approved Paramount’s proposed takeover, converting yesterday’s pre-vote uncertainty into a post-vote execution story centered on integration, regulatory sequencing, and governance.

## What’s New

- Shareholders approved Paramount’s acquisition proposal in Thursday’s special vote.
- Coverage from CNBC reports that shareholders separately rejected a CEO payout proposal tied to the transaction.
- This shifts the near-term risk profile from vote outcome uncertainty to closing conditions and post-merger execution.
