# Update: Pentagon expands classified AI contracts to multiple cloud and model vendors

The Pentagon’s classified AI procurement appears to be broadening from a single-vendor storyline into a multi-vendor buildout spanning cloud infrastructure and model providers.

## What’s New
- New reports indicate additional classified AI agreements involving major vendors beyond the previously reported Google deal.
- Coverage now points to participation by infrastructure and model ecosystem players including Nvidia, Microsoft/AWS, and OpenAI/Google in defense-focused AI programs.
- The shift suggests the Pentagon is scaling from exploratory contracting toward a more diversified classified AI stack.

## Why it matters
A multi-vendor posture can reduce concentration risk, improve workload fit across mission types, and accelerate deployment options inside secure environments—while raising integration and oversight complexity.
