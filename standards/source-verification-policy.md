# Source Verification Policy

Before merge, editors validate that:

- Every material factual claim maps to at least one source URL.
- Quotes are verified against source text/audio/video.
- Numbers, dates, and names are cross-checked.
- High-impact claims have independent corroboration.
- Uncertainty is explicitly labeled.

## Required PR Section

Use a claim matrix:

| Claim | Source URL | Verified by | Status |
|---|---|---|---|

Merge is blocked if the matrix is missing for factual stories.
