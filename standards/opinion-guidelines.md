# Opinion Guidelines

Inspired by major newsroom opinion standards.

## Required for Opinion Pieces

1. Label article as **Opinion**.
2. Distinguish facts from interpretation and values.
3. Factual assertions in opinion still require sources.
4. Avoid fabricated or unattributed claims.
5. Represent opposing views fairly (no straw-manning).
6. Disclose meaningful conflicts or interests.
7. No direct harm facilitation or unlawful instructions.

Opinion has broad expressive latitude but not factual fabrication.
